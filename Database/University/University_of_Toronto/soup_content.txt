<h2>Actuarial Science</h2>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="container" id="main" role="main">
<p class="campus-block">St. George Campus, Faculty of Arts &amp; Science</p>
<div class="row">
<div class="col-md-8">
</div>
<div class="col-md-6">
<h2>Program Information</h2>
<p>Degree(s):
<strong>Honours Bachelor of Science</strong>
</p>
<p>Program(s):
<strong>Actuarial Science																																																		(Major, Specialist)</strong><br/>
</p>
<p>OUAC Admission Code:
<strong>TPG (Physical &amp; Mathematical Sciences)</strong></p>
</div>
<div class="col-md-6">
<h2>Academic Requirements</h2>
<p>Ontario Secondary School Diploma<br/>Six 4U/M courses, including:</p>
<ul>
<li>Calculus &amp; Vectors (MCV4U)</li>
<li>English (ENG4U)</li>
</ul>
<p>Find equivalent requirements for <a href="/apply/requirements/canadian-high-school-canadian-university-and-college/">Canadian high school systems</a>, <a href="/apply/requirements/international-high-school-secondary/usa/">US high school system</a>, <a href="/apply/requirements/international-high-school-secondary/ib/">International Baccalaureate</a>, <a href="/apply/requirements/international-high-school-secondary/british/">British-Patterned Education</a>, <a href="/apply/requirements/international-high-school-secondary/french/">French-Patterned Education</a>, <a href="/apply/requirements/international-high-school-secondary/cape/">CAPE</a>, and other <a href="/apply/requirements/international-high-school-secondary/">international high school systems</a>.</p>
</div>
